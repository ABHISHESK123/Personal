Our Story

TAYSHETE INDUSTRIES PRIVATE LIMITED (TIPL) is the company which has history of past 30 years TIP is the updated and restructured company from the Shankar Earthmovers and Kamlai Earthmovers. The company is formed by the considering and fulfilling the opportunities and needs of the upcoming market. The company run by the third generation of family which are wall qualified in their fields. They decided to start new company with new ideas and with more working abarties and capacities. From the post years of experience and excellence in same field we are very sure about our great performance and services We at (TIP), own a range of heavy earthmoving equipments.

TAYASHETE's EQUIPMENT

TAYASHETE's is a company that offers a wide range of machines for various construction and earthmoving purposes. They have a diverse selection of equipment, including diggers, bulldozers, and trucks.Their diggers come in different sizes, ranging from 3-tonne to 30-tonne.

These machines are designed to excavate and move large amounts of earth and materials efficiently.In addition to diggers, TAYASHETE's also provides bulldozers, which are used for pushing and leveling soil, rocks, and debris. Their bulldozers range from 13-tonne to 23-tonne, offering flexibility in handling different types of construction projects.
